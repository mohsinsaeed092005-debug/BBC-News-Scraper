# BBC News Scraper – Advanced Version

This project scrapes the BBC News website and collects news articles automatically.
It saves the data in CSV format with the following columns:

- **SERIAL NUMBER** – Unique number for each article
- **ARTICLE** – Full text of the news article
- **TITLE** – Headline of the news article
- **GENRE** – Category of news (News, Sport, Technology, Health, etc.)
- **DATE** – Date when the article was scraped

---

## Files in this repository

| File | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `scrape_bbc_news.py` | Python script to scrape BBC News articles |
